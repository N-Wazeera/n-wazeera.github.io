About Me

Hi! I’m Nurul Wazeera — a computer science student passionate about blending technology with artistry. I love working on projects that merge logic and imagination, whether it’s building interactive visuals, animating 3D scenes, or creating something tactile like crochet.

My academic journey has introduced me to the world of graphics, animation, and multimedia, while my hobbies remind me to keep things playful and personal. I enjoy experimenting with color, form, and storytelling — both digitally and by hand.

When I’m not coding or creating, you’ll probably find me sketching ideas, crocheting plush creatures, or just watching the moon — because that’s when I feel most inspired.
